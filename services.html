What We Deliver

Not just images.
A buildable package.

Every Astro Station design closes with a complete, organized project package, documented down to the last detail.

It is the difference between a beautiful picture and a buildable house: a package your general contractor can price, your vendors can order from, and your bidding team can take to market, without a single follow-up question to fill a gap.

01
Design Concept & Renderings
The complete design intent: photoreal interior, exterior and aerial visualizations of every key space.
02
Plans, Sections & Elevations
Full architectural drawing set: floor plans, building sections, exterior and interior elevations, roof and site plans.
03
Construction Documents
Coordinated, dimensioned documentation ready for permitting and submission to a general contractor.
04
Material Selections
Curated palettes for every room: stone, wood, tile, metal, paint and fabric, presented with samples and references.
05
Finish Schedules
Room-by-room finish schedules, tile layouts, door and window schedules, every surface accounted for.
06
Millwork & Casework Details
Shop-level drawings for every built-in, kitchen, vanity and panel, fabrication-ready for Dragon Millwork or any shop.
07
Materials List & Vendor Info
A complete purchasing list: quantities, specifications, vendors, and where to buy every material in the project.
08
Bid & Pricing Ready
Documentation structured for competitive bidding, your GC and pricing team work from one organized source.
