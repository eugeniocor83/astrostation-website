01 — The Studio

One continuous practice.

Astro Station is a studio of architects, designers, engineers and builders working as one. The architect who draws your home sits at the same table as the contractor who builds it, from first sketch to keys.

02 — The Model

The handoff is where homes go wrong. So we removed it.

For decades, residential work has been split across trades: the architect draws, the designer specifies, the contractor improvises. Detail is lost at every handoff.

We rebuilt the pipeline as a single practice: one project manager, one set of standards, one contract, one finished home.

04 — The Crew

A small senior crew, accountable for every coordinate.

Three principals carry every project: David Castrillón (Founder & Principal: holds the pen on every engagement), Maria Bernal (Principal, Interior Design: sets the material register), and Eu Correa (Principal, Strategy, keeps the studio's standards and systems coherent). José Martínez, GC and engineer, leads our construction arm, Astro Cósmico.

Internally, we call ourselves cosmonauts: building a home is a long mission, and we fly it with a small crew in full command of the systems.

05 — Promises

Three commitments. No asterisks.

Open-book budget

Every cost visible from day one, updated weekly, built with you in the room.

Weekly transparency

Daily site photos, written weekly reports, a client portal where every decision is logged.

Lifetime support

Two-year structural warranty, annual maintenance program. The handover starts the relationship.

06 — Deliverables

Not just images. A buildable package.

Every design closes with a complete, priced-and-permitted-ready package: plans, construction documents, millwork details, finish schedules, vendor lists. The difference between a beautiful picture and a buildable house.

We hand it over as one considered object, what we call the White Box: everything a home needs to be built, resolved and gathered into a single set.
