## How to review

Walk through the site in this order: **Home → Work → About → Services → Contact**. As you go, jot notes by section using the format below so we can triage feedback quickly.


## What we're looking for

### 1. Tone & narrative
- Does the writing sound like Astro — calm, confident, editorial?
- Anything that reads as marketing copy or filler?
- Are the project concept narratives distinct from one another, or do they blur?

### 2. Brand & visual consistency
- Logo (asterisk + wordmark) reads correctly on every page?
- Black/white palette feels seamless — no off-grays or accidental cream blocks?
- Typography hierarchy holds (Saira display / Inter body / JetBrains Mono labels)?

### 3. Factual accuracy
- **Leadership:** David Castrillón (Founder & Principal), Maria Bernal (Principal, Interiors), Eu Correa (Principal), José Martínez (Director of Construction)
- **Studios:** Coconut Grove · Miami · Los Angeles
- **Divisions:** Design Studio · Astro Cósmico (construction) · Dragon Division (millwork) · Interiors
- **Contact:** correct emails (.com), correct phone, correct address

### 4. Work / portfolio
- Project names spelled correctly
- Photography matches the right project
- Concept narrative captures the actual design intent (flag anything that misrepresents the project)
- Missing projects to add? Projects to remove?

### 5. Services
- Scope descriptions for Architecture, Interiors, Construction, Millwork are accurate
- Anything we offer that's missing? Anything listed we don't actually do?

### 6. About
- Story chapter reads true to the firm's actual origin
- Practice / Universe / Promises chapters reflect how we actually work

### 7. Functional
- Any broken links, missing images, or pages that won't load?
- Mobile: does the site hold up on your phone?
