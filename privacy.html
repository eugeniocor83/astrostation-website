Privacy Policy.

How Astro Station collects, uses and protects the information you share with us through this website. Last updated June 2026.

This Privacy Policy explains what information Astro Station ("Astro Station," "we," "us" or "our") collects when you visit astrostation.com, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using this website, you agree to the practices described below.

01 — Information We Collect

What we may collect.

We collect information in two ways: information you provide directly, and information collected automatically as you browse.

Information you provide. When you submit our contact form, request a consultation or otherwise reach out, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, project location, estimated budget, target timeline and any details you choose to include about your project. When you contact us by email or telephone, we receive the information in that correspondence.

Information collected automatically. When you visit the site, we and our service providers may automatically collect limited technical and usage information, such as your IP address, browser type, device type, referring page, the pages you view and the time and date of your visit. This information is collected through cookies and similar technologies, and only to the extent permitted by your cookie choices.

02 — Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Cookies and consent.

Cookies are small text files placed on your device that help a website function and understand how it is used. We use a limited set of cookies and similar technologies, grouped into three categories:

Strictly necessary. Required for the site to operate, such as remembering your cookie preferences and enabling secure form submission. These are always active and do not require consent.

Analytics. Help us understand how visitors interact with the site so we can improve it. We use Google Analytics, a service provided by Google LLC, which sets cookies and processes limited usage data on our behalf. IP addresses are anonymized, and Google’s Consent Mode is used so analytics only run after you accept or enable them. For more information, see Google’s Privacy & Terms.

Marketing. May be used to measure the performance of campaigns. These run only after you accept or enable them.

When you first visit, a consent banner lets you Accept, Decline, or Manage Preferences. Non-essential cookies do not load until you have given consent. You can change your choices at any time using the Cookie Preferences link in the footer of every page.

03 — How We Use Information

How we use it.

We use the information we collect to respond to your inquiries and consultation requests, to provide and discuss our services, to operate and improve the website, to understand site traffic and performance, to communicate with you about your project, and to comply with our legal and professional obligations. We do not sell your personal information.

04 — How Information May Be Shared

Service providers.

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share information with trusted service providers who perform functions on our behalf, such as website hosting, form delivery, email and analytics. These providers are permitted to use the information only to provide services to us and are expected to keep it confidential. We may also disclose information where required by law, to protect our rights, or in connection with a business transfer.

05 — Data Retention & Security

Retention and security.

We retain personal information for as long as needed to respond to your inquiry, provide our services and meet legal requirements, after which it is deleted or anonymized. We use reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect the information we hold, though no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

06 — Your Privacy Rights

Your rights.

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access the personal information we hold about you, to request correction or deletion, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below.

California residents. Under the California Consumer Privacy Act, as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act, you have the right to know what personal information we collect and how it is used, the right to request deletion, the right to correct inaccurate information, and the right to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information. We do not sell or share personal information as those terms are defined under California law. We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of these rights.

07 — Children's Privacy

Children.

This website is intended for a general, professional audience and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us and we will delete it.

08 — Changes to This Policy

Updates.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Material changes will be reflected on this page, and your continued use of the site after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

09 — Contact Us

Privacy requests.

For any question or request regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal information, contact us at info@astrostation.com or +1 786 909 1565. We are based in Miami, Florida and Los Angeles, California.
