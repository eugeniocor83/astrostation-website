03 — Process

From shop drawing to install.

Every millwork item moves through the same four-stage path. A direct line from sketch to install: no intermediaries, no specification drift.

Stage 01

Shop Drawings

Detailed working drawings prepared in-house. Joinery, hardware, finish samples, coordinated with the architecture and interiors team before fabrication.

Stage 02

Material Selection

Wood species, veneer matching, hardware, stone and finish samples reviewed and approved alongside the client. Mock-ups for high-stakes elements.

Stage 03

In-Shop Fabrication

Built in our own shop by our own team. CNC, hand finishing and pre-assembly under quality control before anything reaches the site.

Stage 04

On-Site Install

Delivered and installed by the same crew that built it. Punch and final commissioning under the same warranty as the rest of the house.

Cross-team

Coordinated with Architecture

Wall thicknesses, lighting, mechanicals and structure coordinated with David Castrillón's drawing set from day one, never patched in.

Cross-team

Coordinated with Construction
