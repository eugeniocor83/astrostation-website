Construction, by us.

Astro Cósmico is the general-contracting arm of Astro Station. Custom builds, whole-home renovations and owner's representation: executed by our own team, under one contract, on the same drawings we drew.

03 — Engagement

Three phases. One promise.

Astro Cósmico operates in a three-phase model. Open-book budgeting. Weekly transparency. Lifetime support after the keys turn.

Phase 01

Pre-Construction

Listening, drawing review, schedule construction, value engineering, open-book budget assembly with full sub-trade pricing, assembled with the client in the room.

  • Client & architect listening
  • Schedule & logistics plan
  • Open-book budget
  • Sub-trade pricing & approval
  • Permit strategy
Phase 02

Construction

Site execution under in-house leadership. Dedicated PM, daily photo logs, weekly written reports, real-time client portal access. Self-performed millwork.

  • Site mobilization
  • Self-performed millwork (Dragon)
  • Sub-contractor management
  • Quality & safety control
  • Real-time reporting
Phase 03

Post-Construction

Walkthrough, commissioning, owner training, home-operations guide. The structure stays our project after the keys turn.

  • Final walkthrough
  • Home-operations guide
  • Two-year structural warranty
  • Annual maintenance program
  • Lifetime referral support
04 — Capabilities

What Astro Cósmico delivers.

→ A Custom Home Builds Ground-up residential construction. Concrete to keys, fully managed by an Astro Cósmico PM working directly with the in-house architects.
→ B Whole-Home Renovations Strategic gut and reposition projects: restoring structure, modernizing systems, reframing the architecture without losing the soul.
→ C Owner's Representation For clients building elsewhere. Astro Cósmico acts as your owner-side advocate, managing third-party contractors, budgets and quality.

Pools, hardscape and lighting are sequenced inside Astro Cósmico site schedules but engaged as separate scopes, see Landscape.
